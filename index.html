About Usüè´

Loyola School is a prestigious institution run by the Jesuit Society, and we believe not just in mere education, but the all-round development of the students.Ensconced on an extensive campus on the outskirts of the capital city and provided with the best infrastructure and assisted by a committed faculty, it offers the ideal atmosphere for the holistic development of about 1750 students of LKG to Plus Two classes in the ICSE, ISC and CBSE streams. Our motto is "Magis" which means to strive for excellence. We enable Loyolites to do exactly this.There are a number of clubs and forums functioning in this school to foster personal and inter-personal skills as well as social consciousness.With a view to improving and testing the leadership qualities and organizational prowess of the senior students, the school has been supporting, since 1996, their initiative in hosting prestigious inter-school events like La Fest. Loyola Basketball Tournament conducted every year since 1974 is meant to promote sportsman spirit, and the Loyolite hoopsters have lifted the trophy sixteen times so far.
